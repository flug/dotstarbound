To install, you just need to move take the 'hlhevsuit' folder this readme is inside and move or copy it into your Starbound/mods folder.

Imitation H.E.V gear can be made at the anvil
Legitimate H.E.V gear can be made at the robotic crafting table
Physicist's Glasses can be made at a crafting table
  
Voila. You can now cosplay as everyone's favorite ass-kicking mute. Have fun!
